Cash In on Justice with Bail Office Bonuses

Get double rewards on bounty targets and staff income, 3X GTA$ and RP in the new Community Series, and more!

Dec 5, 2024

Lay down the law by rounding up felons and earn 2X GTA$ and RP for bringing Standard Bounty Targets to justice in GTA Online. Secure three Bounty Targets of any kind to complete the Weekly Challenge and also pocket an extra GTA$100,000.

Chomping at the bit to get after these bail jumpers? Head to Maze Bank Foreclosures and purchase a Bail Office at 40% off through December 9. All Upgrades and Modifications are also 40% off, so now is a perfect time to add that extra staff member or armor plating for the Bottom Dollar bounty wagon.

2X GTA$ Bail Office Staff Income

The Bail Office’s staff is here to aid in your bounty hunting efforts and also help pad your pockets. Collect 2X GTA$ on Bail Office Staff Income through December 9. Purchase Agent Upgrades from Maze Bank Foreclosures at 40% off, then pay them a visit at their desks inside your Bail Office to put 'em to work.

Heist Challenge Update

The community has eclipsed GTA$16T towards the unprecedented GTA$20 TRILLION goal in The Heist Challenge, but as of today it's still short of the task.

Considering how ambitious the mark is, and how close you've already come, we've decided to extend the challenge and gift anyone who completes an eligible Heist Finale as part of the challenge the Pacific Standard Varsity Jacket, delivered within 72 hours of login this week.

Log in and play GTA Online anytime between today and December 9 to claim the Jacket, and keep pushing on those Heist Finales to get to GTA$20 TRILLION and claim the brand-new Police Predator Boat and the NOOSE Law Enforcement Outfit as part of GTA Online: Agents of Sabotage.

3X GTA$ and RP on the Community Series

Winter is nearing, and the Community Series features a thrilling showcase of Races and Deathmatches inspired by the frosty season. Participating in these unique and inventive creations that make the most of the cold will reward you with 3X GTA$ and RP through December 9.

Reservoir Ice Race 2 by MrMacHenry
Glide your way to victory on an icy track high above the Land Act Reservoir. Skillfully maneuver tight hairpin turns, a bustling cross-over, and challenging snowy conditions in this thrilling winter Stunt Race.

[D4D] Winter Rally by Drummer_4D
Success awaits those with an outdoorsy spirit in this rugged Stunt Race. Navigate snow-covered off-road terrain filled with ramps, sharp turns, and a finish line marked by a giant, eye-catching blimp.

w_Suicidal Toboggan Run by wimpendahl
Race through tunnels and kick up a trail of snowy dust in this adrenaline-fueled Stunt Race with wallrides, upside-down tracks, suspended platforms, and high-flying jumps that could send you plummeting to an untimely end.

Satan Claws v Santa Claus by GTA_RRRacing
Channel the naughty and the nice as you smash through barriers placed in the mouths of giant Santa figurines using the Rocket Voltic’s boost.

Beyond The Ice Wall by ManOfFire
Navigate a snow-covered battlefield dotted with pyramids for ample cover and armed with the Up-n-Atomizer whose laser blasts add colorful bursts to the grayscale landscape in this intense Team Deathmatch.

¬Flens’ Snow Temple by OldFlens
This Team Deathmatch is based around a large platform that players can either shoot through or climb to gain a height advantage. Use the knife pickup to bank extra points for each kill.

Snow Hotring by Nightdragon88
Charge ahead of competitors without losing control — the winter weather guarantees more slipping and sliding on this snow-covered Hotring track.

Premium Deluxe Motorsport Showroom

Drop by local legend Simeon Yetarian at his Premium Deluxe Motorsport showroom to check out his latest vehicle lineup, featuring a sleek sedan with a limited time, retro-inspired livery, and more:

  • Enus Stafford (Sedan, 30% off) in the Oh Enus! limited-time livery

  • Progen Tyrus (Super, 30% off)

  • Weeny Dynasty (Sports Classic, 30% off)

  • Dinka Thrust (Motorcycle)

  • Invetero Coquette BlackFin (Muscle)
