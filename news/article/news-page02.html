GTA+ Members Are Treated to Special Gifts This Halloween Season

Claim a Free HVY Nightshark, Halloween Apparel, and Much More

Oct 3, 2024

GTA+ Members can traverse the haunted highways of San Andreas this Halloween season in a free HVY Nightshark weaponized off-roader adorned with the rare Skulls livery. Members will also enjoy a range of festive apparel, exclusive bonuses, special discounts, and more.

Along with all the latest benefits outlined below, keep an eye on the Rockstar Newswire through November 6 for exclusive bonuses and surprises available only to GTA+ Members.

The HVY Nightshark

If you can’t make people love you, make them fear you from behind the wheel of the HVY Nightshark (Off-Road) decked out in the chilling Skulls livery. This SUV features dual machine guns and enough armor plating for keeping this season’s nightmarish adversaries at bay.

GTA+ Members can claim their HVY Nightshark for free at The Vinewood Car Club showroom.

Spooky Styles

Alongside traditional Halloween tricks, all GTA+ Members can also enjoy a heaping portion of treats: Get the Demon Biker Jacket, Blushed Cracked Puppet Mask, and Pale Stitched Mask through November 6 for a spooktacular look.

New Chameleon Paints

Ride under the cover of darkness in an ominous shade of deep blue-green hues with Dark Teal Pearl Chameleon Paint and Oil Slick Pearl Chameleon Wheel Paint to boot.

Halloween Adversary Mode Bonuses

Face off in a frightful rotation of Halloween Adversary Modes awarding 2X GTA$ and RP to all players through November 6. GTA+ Members will have their bonuses stacked up to 4X GTA$ and RP on these featured Halloween Adversary Modes, so keep up to date with the Rockstar Newswire each week to check out what’s on.

Double Rewards on Business Battles and Dispatch Work

Earn Double Rewards by boosting Los Santos’ economy and tackling Business Battles, where you’ll get 2X GTA$, RP, and Goods. Plus, hit the road in your Law Enforcement Vehicle to take on Dispatch Work for 2X GTA$ and RP.

Discounts

Reinvigorated corpses aren't the only things emerging from the subterranean depths of GTA Online. GTA+ Members can kickstart their Gunrunning operations with 40% off Bunker Properties at Maze Bank Foreclosures.

Scourge the streets with 50% off the Albany Brigham (Muscle), Albany Lurcher (Muscle), and the LCC Sanctus (Motorcycle) — all decked out in Halloween liveries.

Plus, gear up with Body Armor at 50% off, and enjoy an additional 20% discount on it at the Gun Van, which is always blipped on the map for GTA+ Members.

And More…

GTA+ Members also get access to 100-vehicle storage capacity of The Vinewood Club Garage, the selection of classic Rockstar Games titles in the GTA+ Games Library, special GTA+ Shark Cards with an extra 15% GTA$ bonus, and much more. For full details, visit the GTA+ website.
