Celebrate Thanksgiving Season in Red Dead Online with Trader Bonuses

Double Rewards on Trader Sales, Boosted Payouts for Posses, Get Limited-Time Clothing and Outlaw Pass Items, and More

Dec 5, 2024

Traders play a key role during this time of year across the frontier, supplying pelts, hides, and more to ensure the warmth and joy of the holiday season as winter approaches. This month, Traders can look forward to boosted rewards and exclusive bonuses for keeping their inventories well-stocked and contributing to the season of unity, gratitude, and abundance.

Stock up on Perfect Carcasses, Pelts, Skins, and Hides to earn 2X Trader Materials — just in time for the onset of colder weather. Sell your goods and enjoy 2X RDO$, XP, and Role XP on all Trader Sales.

Plus, feast your eyes on a selection of returning Limited-Time Clothing, a new Featured Series, and a monthlong lineup of rewards from previous Outlaw Passes.

2X GTA$ Bail Office Staff Income

The Bail Office’s staff is here to aid in your bounty hunting efforts and also help pad your pockets. Collect 2X GTA$ on Bail Office Staff Income through December 9. Purchase Agent Upgrades from Maze Bank Foreclosures at 40% off, then pay them a visit at their desks inside your Bail Office to put 'em to work.

2X Ability Card XP and Gold on Featured Series

Join this month’s Featured Series, featuring four weeks of action-packed Showdown modes culminating in a Hardcore Showdown finale. Earn 2X Ability Card XP and Gold along the way, making it the perfect chance to boost your rank and pocket some extra rewards through December 2.

  • November 5 – 11: Tumbleweed Series

  • November 12 – 18: Ambarino Series

  • November 19 – 25: Lemoyne Series

  • November 26 – December 2: Hardcore Showdown Series

Plus, win in the Featured Series this month to receive the Dunster Shirt.

Limited-Time Clothing Items Return

Frontier fashionistas in need of a wardrobe refresh can peruse an expansive list of Limited-Time Clothing returning to Red Dead Online this month, available for purchase from participating Tailors or the Wheeler, Rawson & Co. Catalogue through December 2:

The Danube Outfit | The Tasman Outfit | Gator Hat | Owanjila Hat | Patterned Bandana | Cardozo Vest | Ortega Vest | Morning Tail Coat | Macbay Jacket | Prieto Poncho | And More…

Weekly and Monthly Rewards

Traders who log in to play Red Dead Online will receive 20 Small Game Arrows and 3 Potent Miracle Tonics through December 2. Elsewhere, the Persistent Posse setup fee is waived through December 2, and a returning set of Outlaw Pass items are available as rewards for completing this month’s weekly activities.

Reach Plentiful Material Status in your Trader Camp this month to receive the Fake Beard and Craft an Item at your Campfire to receive the Goldwing Spurs.

  • November 5 – 11: Complete a Trader Sale to receive the Faucheux Gloves

  • November 12 – 18: Complete your Trader Daily Challenges for three days in a row to receive the Clovelly Pants

  • November 19 – 25: Complete a Trader Sale to receive the Pittman Shirt

  • November 26 – December 2: Rank up five times or reach Rank 20 as a Trader to receive the Amador Hat

Complete all four of the weekly incentives above to receive the New Threads Emote.
